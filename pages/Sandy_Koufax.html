Sandy Koufax

Sanford Koufax ( / ˈ k oʊ f æ k s / ; born Sanford Braun ; December 30, 1935) is a former American Major League Baseball (MLB) left-handed pitcher . He pitched 12 seasons for the Brooklyn/Los Angeles Dodgers , from 1955 to 1966. Koufax, at age 36 in 1972, became the youngest player ever elected to the Baseball Hall of Fame . [1] He has been hailed as one of the greatest pitchers in baseball history.
